Design Your Life was founded by graduates of the Stanford University course who found that applying design principles to everyday life can have profound effects. The desire to bting this knowledge to everyone led us to build our app. Enjoy!

Since the start, our team has been made up of our core group of graduates from the course. Today, we have expanded to include front-end developers, React developsers, and UX design professionals. Get to know us here!

The Standford Life Design Lab applies desing thinking to tackling the "wicked" problems of life and vocational wayfinding. Our app is the most convinient way to access the benefits of this paradigm.
